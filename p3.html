Biography

Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreuer and inventor. He was the cofounder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s, along wiht Apple co-founder Steve Wzniak. Creative entrepreneur whose passion for perfection and ferocius drive revolutionized six industries: personal computers, animated movies, music, phones, tablet computing, and digital publishing.
